About the TechWiserX 2026 AI & Cyber Series Europe

The TechWiserX 2026 AI & Cyber Series Europe is an exclusive, invite-only event designed to bring together senior leaders from key industries, including financial services, banking, insurance, manufacturing, retail, healthcare, and automotive.

Hosted across the UK, Dach, and the Benelux region, TechWiserX 2026 AI & Cyber Series Europe delivers cutting-edge cybersecurity content curated by the most influential industry experts. It offers unparalleled networking opportunities and a platform for pre-qualified meetings with leading solution providers, fostering meaningful discussions and actionable strategies to tackle the evolving cyber threat landscape.
